Plugins Directory » Help Desk

Author: Petr Pospisil
Website: http://www.easyredmine.com/online-store/easy-redmine-plugins/help-desk
Code repository: -
Registered on: 2013-01-14 (20 days)
Current version: 1.0.1
Compatible with: Redmine 2.2.x, 2.1.x, 2.0.x, 1.4.x

Solution for automation of your support processes. 4 easy steps lead to fully automated support system within your Easy Redmine. This plugin is distributed together with Alerts - Early Warning System (http://www.redmine.org/plugins/alerts-early-warning-system) - it thus provides e-mail notifications, when SLA of particular support project is violated.

Set you Redmine Help Desk in 4 Easy Steps:

1) define mailboxes to be withdrawn - you can have as many mailboxes as you need¶

2) define e-mail templates for automatic replies and notification about ticked updates¶

3) define support projects - particular domain or sender e-mail address is mapped on particulart support project¶

4) define SLA - priorities according to key words in e-mail subject & time to solve support tickes¶

Help Desk Workflow¶

Help Desk tickets have due date and due time

While updating the help desk ticket, you can utilize e-mail templates:

Check option "copy of update to e-mail"

Than you can edit an e-mail template or send it right off-hand

Warnings¶

There are 2 default warning which come with Help Desk.

1) Alert of SLA violation - 75% of time to solve the ticket is over and ticket is untouched
2) Alarm of SLA violation - 100% of time to solve the ticket is over and ticket is untouched
